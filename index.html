Banknotes (11th Series) - Detailed Visual Guide

Coins (2017/2020 Series)

රු 20
7-LOBED

**Shape:** Seven-lobed.

**Visual:** CBSL Head Office Building on the front.

**Metal:** Nickel Plated Steel / Aluminium Bronze.

රු 10
11-LOBED

**Shape:** Eleven-lobed (Undulating edge).

**Visual:** Armorial Ensign of Sri Lanka.

**Size:** Largest diameter (26.4 mm) of the current stainless steel coins.

රු 5
ROUND

**Shape:** Round with Milled Edge (regular indentations).

**Visual:** Armorial Ensign of Sri Lanka.

**Material:** Stainless Steel. Thickness: 1.8mm.

රු 2
ROUND

**Shape:** Round with Notched Edge.

**Visual:** Armorial Ensign of Sri Lanka.

**Size:** 22.0 mm diameter.

රු 1
ROUND

**Shape:** Round with Intermitted Milled Edge.

**Visual:** Armorial Ensign of Sri Lanka.

**Size:** Smallest diameter (20.0 mm).

Note: Cents (25c, 50c) are generally not in circulation.
